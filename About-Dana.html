About Dana

Dana Plyler earned her Bachelor’s degree from the University of Redlands, and her Master’s in Social Work from the University of Southern California. She served as an intern at Jewish Family Service of Los Angeles in their domestic violence shelter where she provided therapy to survivors of domestic violence and their children. Additionally, she has several years of experience providing individual and family therapy to children with serious emotional disturbance (SED) and adults with severe mental illness, both in English and in Spanish.
